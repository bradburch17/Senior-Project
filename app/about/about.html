About the Website

This website was designed by Brad Burch for his senior seminar project in the Fall of 2016. My inspiration was from running Cross Counry and Track and Field at DePauw University, where they used an outdated website, called LogARun. The purpose of this website is to create a new activity logging website with wearable technology integration. This website will feature FitBit integration, although a further application of this idea would include integration from GPS technology and oher wearable technologies. Even further applications of this website may also include better logging for non-distance athletes, such as sprinters, and graphical analysis of runs. For more information about what could be improved upon, you can email me at joggersandloggers@gmail.com.
