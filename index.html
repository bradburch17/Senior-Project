Welcome to Joggers and Loggers

An activity logging website for runners, by a runner

This is a website for you to log your daily exercise activities in a new way! Create an account to begin logging your daily activities.

This website is made to log your daily activities, no matter your skill level. There is FitBit integration built into the website, so you can easily log your FitBit data to your log!

From the casual exerciser, collegiate athlete, and beyond, wearable technology has become a staple for most people. This website was made in hopes of collecting information from wearable technology to be used in a more useful way for individuals to better understand their training and how much they are exerting themselves.
